About the Euroleague

The Euroleague is Europe's top-tier professional basketball competition, founded in 1958. Over the decades, it has grown into one of the most prestigious basketball leagues globally, featuring elite clubs from across the continent. The league is renowned for its competitive spirit, showcasing intense rivalries and high-quality basketball that captivates fans worldwide.
